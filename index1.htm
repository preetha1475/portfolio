Living Conditions Across Indian Households

Census of India 2011 ¬∑ Tableau Visual Analytics

This project analyzes household living conditions across Indian states using Census of India 2011 Housing & Amenities data.

The goal was to understand regional disparities in access to basic amenities such as electricity, sanitation, drinking water, and overall household infrastructure.

The project was designed as a multi-dashboard Tableau analysis focusing on visual storytelling, inequality detection, and policy-relevant insights.

‚ö° Electricity Access Across Indian Households

Objective

Identify regional inequality in electricity access and highlight states contributing most to electricity deprivation.

Key Insight

While most states show high electricity access, a small number of high-household states contribute disproportionately to electricity deprivation, following a Pareto (80/20) pattern.

üö∞ Water & Sanitation Inequality

Objective

Analyze sanitation access and drinking water quality rather than just availability.

Key Insight

Several states show high drinking water availability but low sanitation access, highlighting a policy gap between water supply and sanitation infrastructure.
