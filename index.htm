Living Conditions Across Indian Households

Census of India 2011 ¬∑ Tableau Visual Analytics

This project analyzes household living conditions across Indian states using Census of India 2011 Housing & Amenities data.

The goal was to understand regional disparities in access to basic amenities such as electricity, sanitation, drinking water, and overall household infrastructure.

The project was designed as a multi-dashboard Tableau analysis focusing on visual storytelling, inequality detection, and policy-relevant insights.
